More about London Pub Standards

Pub Standards is a regular get-together for web folk. Unlike some savagely bigoted groups madly intent on ripping the web community apart, Pub Standards is open to any— and everyone and is as welcoming as a suicide cult recruitment camp.

Started in 2005 and still going strong, the London Chapter of Pub Standards is the original, and still the best place in London to enjoy a tasty beverage with like-minded web people.
