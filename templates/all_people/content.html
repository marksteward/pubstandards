Attended Pub Standards? Get yourself added to this list by doing the following:

  1. Add a photo of yourself to the chapter flickr pool.
  2. Set the photo's title to what you want your name displayed as.
  3. Add a description, if you want one.
  4. Tag the photo with pubstandards:twitter=YOUR_TWITTER_NAME if you want.
